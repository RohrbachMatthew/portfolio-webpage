About Me

Hello! My name is Matthew Rohrbach, and I am an aspiring programmer with a passion for growth and learning. My career journey has been diverse, spanning roles in retail at Walmart, fast food establishments, and primarily factory work. These experiences have shaped my work ethic and adaptability. Seeking a more fulfilling and dynamic career path, I made the decision to pursue a degree in computer science, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the tech industry."

Since I started my journey I have enjoyed learning the fundamentals of Python and MySQL, along with intermediate skills in libraries such as Pandas and Matplotlib. My focus has recently shifted towards data, particularly databases. Although I do not have professional achievements in this field yet, I have dedicated significant time to learning and honing my skills both in school and on my own time.
